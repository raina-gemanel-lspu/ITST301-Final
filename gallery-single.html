Team-Building | March 2022

A continuing process called team building aids in the development of a work group's sense of unity. The team members accept one another's individual characteristics and trust and support one another in addition to having similar expectations for completing work as a group.

The purpose of team building activities is to motivate your people to work together, to develop their strengths, and to address any weaknesses. So, any team building exercise should encourage collaboration rather than competition.
